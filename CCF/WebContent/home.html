This is a website for a Super Smash Brother's Melee tournament that is held in Athens, GA.
It goes by the name Classic City Fisticuffs.
It is hosted every other Saturday at 2:00pm at the Pizza Hut on Baxter Street.
There is a $5 entry fee, and a 50:30:20 split of the pot.
There are both doubles and singles tournaments.
